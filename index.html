About Me

My full name is Jeanny Ann Mytha Tee-Sy. You can call me Jeanny (pronounced as Jee-nie) or JAM which is an acronym of my three Names. I'm an expert in the customer support space, mostly in tech. I also have significant experience in tech writing and knowledge base management.

I got married, and having a baby was a struggle for us, so I had to find a work-life balance and started looking for opportunities that would allow me to work from home. Wanting to work remotely led me to make a career shift and pursue a career in the tech industry.

Home Maker

Outside of work, I am a homemaker and full-time mom to two kids. You know that person who makes the house feel like a home? That's me! I'm the one who's always hustling to keep things tidy, well-fed, and running smoothly.

I love finding creative solutions to everyday challenges, whether it's decluttering a messy closet or figuring out how to make a one-pot wonder for dinner. And let's not forget about the power of a good DIY project - I'm always up for a new challenge!

Content Creator

Being a stay-at-home mom and an extrovert, one of my challenges is that I don't have anyone to share my stories with, so I use social media as an outlet. I share my stories by creating content to inspire and motivate others.

I share a glimpse of my daily life, how I find work-life balance, my recipes, self-care, personal growth, and my faith. From social media posts to videos, my content is always honest and raw, providing a window into my experiences and insights from which others can learn.

Everyone has a story worth sharing, and by sharing our experiences, we can create a community of support and encouragement.
